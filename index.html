About

Hi, I'm little single page application, named 'My colors'. I will take some image that you give me and I'll try to give you some nice background with colors from that image. I use popular libraries: d3 and trianglify

The javascript method that extracts colors from image is gpl licenced and you can use it freely. Fire up inspector and take a look on `mk.com.process`.

You'll get global `window.nodups` var with colors of your image.
